#HAVDALAH#
## Havdalah — The Separation

The Havdalah separates the holy from the ordinary, light from darkness, Israel from the nations, Shabbat from the six days of work.

**Wine**
Blessed are You, Lord our God, King of the universe, who creates the fruit of the vine.

**Spices**
Blessed are You, Lord our God, King of the universe, who creates varieties of spices.

**Candle**
Blessed are You, Lord our God, King of the universe, who creates the lights of fire.

**The Separation**
Blessed are You, Lord our God, King of the universe, who separates the holy from the ordinary, light from darkness, Israel from the nations, the seventh day from the six days of work.
